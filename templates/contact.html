Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I book a hospital bed?

Most bookings are confirmed within 2-4 hours. Emergency cases are prioritized and processed immediately.

Is the booking service free?

Yes, our booking platform is completely free. You only pay the hospital's standard fees.

Do you work with insurance companies?

We partner with hospitals that accept most major insurance plans. Check with the specific hospital for coverage details.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before your scheduled admission without penalty.
